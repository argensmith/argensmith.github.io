  • How secure is this site?
    • Reporting a sighting: The form does not collect email addresses, but your IP may be collected by Google. If you are personally worried, you can protect yourself by using a secure, non-UCSD VPN or submitting on a device owned by someone in less risk.
    • Viewing sightings: This website does not collect any information about visitors. Your DNS provider (usually your wifi provider) can see what links you visit, so using a vpn/securer browser (duck-duck-go) would make this more secure.
    • Viewing sightings on Google Sheets: Here, you will have to be logged in to view the sheet. This is the least secure and should be avoided if you're at all worried. All information on the Google Sheet is shown on this page.
